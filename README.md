### The ORM (Online Reputation Management) Checklist
- [ ] Google yourself and your business monthly
- [ ] Claim and optimize all key social media profiles
- [ ] Set up Google Alerts for your name and brand
- [ ] Audit and clean up old social media content
- [ ] Develop a content creation schedule
- [ ] Monitor review sites relevant to your industry
- [ ] Create positive content to push down negative results

### Crisis Response Protocol
1.  **Assess** - Determine the scope and severity
2.  **Acknowledge** - Respond quickly and appropriately
3.  **Address** - Take concrete action to resolve
4.  **Analyze** - Learn and improve processes
